About Pokhara Avengers

Pokhara Avengers are a professional T20 franchise representing the scenic city of Pokhara in the Nepal Premier League. The team brings the energy of Gandaki Province onto the field, backed by a passionate fan base and a modern franchise setup.

Led by dynamic opener Kushal Bhurtel and guided by head coach Rajiv Kumar, the Avengers combine explosive top-order batting, multi-dimensional all-rounders, and a strong pace–spin bowling unit, making them one of the most entertaining sides in NPL 2025.
