About Sudurpaschim Royals

Sudurpaschim Royals are a professional T20 franchise team representing Sudurpaschim Province in the Nepal Premier League. The franchise was introduced in 2024 as one of the eight original teams in the league.

In the inaugural NPL 2024 season, the Royals finished top of the league stage and reached the final, ending as runners-up after a strong campaign driven by match-winners with both bat and ball. With Dipendra Singh Airee leading on the field and Jagat Tamata guiding from the dugout, Sudurpaschim Royals enter the NPL 2025 season aiming to go one step further and lift the trophy.

Recent Performance

Sudurpaschim Royals were one of the standout sides in the first ever Nepal Premier League in 2024, topping the league stage and finishing as runners-up in the final. With a strong core retained and upgraded overseas signings, they enter NPL 2025 as one of the favourites.

The experience of players like Chris Lynn, Scott Kuggeleijn and Josh Brown, combined with Nepal stars Dipendra Singh Airee, Aarif Sheikh, Binod Bhandari and others, gives the Royals a powerful, balanced lineup in all departments.
