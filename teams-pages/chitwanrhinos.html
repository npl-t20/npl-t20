About Chitwan Rhinos

Chitwan Rhinos are a professional T20 franchise competing in the Nepal Premier League, representing the cricket-loving region of Chitwan. Built for NPL Season 2, the Rhinos bring together a mix of Nepal’s brightest talents and experienced international stars.

The team is led by explosive all-rounder Kushal Malla, with experienced overseas performers like Dawid Malan, Ravi Bopara and Sohail Tanvir providing world-class quality. With powerful batting, clever spin options and left-arm pace, Chitwan Rhinos are designed to be a genuine title contender in NPL 2025.
