About Biratnagar Kings

Biratnagar Kings are a professional T20 franchise representing Biratnagar and the wider Koshi Province in the Nepal Premier League. The franchise joined the league as part of the NPL’s expansion, with M.A.D. Dream Sports securing the ownership rights for the Biratnagar-based team.

Led by star leg-spinner Sandeep Lamichhane, the Kings blend Nepal’s brightest talents with world-class overseas players like Faf du Plessis and Martin Guptill. With a strong bowling core and explosive top order, Biratnagar Kings aim to play fearless, entertaining T20 cricket and bring silverware to the eastern city.

Recent Performance
