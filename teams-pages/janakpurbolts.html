About Janakpur Bolts

Janakpur Bolts are a professional T20 franchise representing the city of Janakpur in the Nepal Premier League (NPL). The team features a strong core of Nepali internationals backed by high–impact overseas signings.

With a top order anchored by wicketkeeper–batter Aasif Sheikh and a versatile group of all–rounders and specialist bowlers, Janakpur Bolts enter the 2025 season as one of the most competitive and well–balanced squads in the league.
